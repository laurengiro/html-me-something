Week in Ireland

Places I Visited:

  • Galway
  • Cliffs of Moher
  • Kilkenny
  • Wicklow Mountains
  • Dublin

Galway

Galway is a coastal town in the west of Ireland. It was a 2 hour bus ride from the Dublin airport. There are many great day trips one could take from Galway including the Cliffs of Moher, the Ashford Castle, and the Aran Islands. Galway has a great pub scene featuring locals who speak Gaelic, which is the traditional language of Ireland, and live bands which play both American pop music as well as traditional Irish tunes.

Cliffs of Moher

The Cliffs of Moher are one of the top tourist attractions in Irelands and several movies were filmed there including The Princess Bride (1987), Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince (2009), and Leap Year (2010).

Kilkenny

Kilkenny is a medieval town in southeast Ireland. Some top attractions include the Kilkenny Castle, Smithwick's Brewery, St. Canice's Cathedral and Round Tower, and The Black Abbey. Kilkenny is also know for its traditional Irish pubs including the Hole in the Wall and Kyteler's Inn. Kyteler's Inn dates back to 1324 and is known for being hauted.

Wicklow Mountians

Less than an hour outside the city of Dublin, you can find the scenic Wicklow Mountians. This is another popular filming location in Ireland for movies and TV shows, including Braveheart (1995), P.S. I Love You (2007), and Vikings (2013-).

Dublin

Dublin is the capital of the Repbulic of Ireland. Main attractions include the Guinness Storehouse, Jameson, Temple Bar, Dublin Castle, St. Patrick's Cathedral, Christ Church Cathedral, Trinity College, St. Stephens Green, and Kilmainham Gaol.
